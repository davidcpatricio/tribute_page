David Bowie

  • 1947 - Born in London, England
  • 1967 - Releases his debut self-titled album
  • 1970 - Marries Mary Angela Barnett
  • 1972 - Makes his first appearence as Ziggy Stardust, which we would become even more famous after the release of the album The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars
  • 1973 - Releases Aladdin Sane
  • 1975 - Introduces his new persona, the Thin White Duke, that would last until the following year
  • 1976 - Moves to West Berlin, Germany
  • 1977 - Releases Low, the first record of the so-called "Berlin Trilogy", which would be followed by "Heroes" in the same year and Lodger in 1979
  • 1983 - Relases Let's Dance, produced by Nile Rodgers
  • 1992 - Marries his second wife, Iman
  • 2007 - Gives his final live performance at WaMu Theater at Madison Square Garden
  • 2013 - Releases The Next Day, his first studio album in a decade
  • 2016 - Dies in New York City at the age of 69, only two days after the release of his final studio album, Blackstar

You can find out more about David Bowie's life and work on his Wikipedia entry.
